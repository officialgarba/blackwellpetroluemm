About Us

BLACKWELL PETROLEUM LIMITED (BPL) was incorporated as a Private Limited indigenous Company to provide Offshore Drilling Operations, Cargo/Rig Inspection and Testing Services, Offshore Support Vessels, Manpower provision, Procurement, in the fields of Oil/Gas services and Government Sector. The Company is part of BUREAU VERITAS Group with offices all over the world.


Our philosophy was focused towards providing technical services in our areas of business to major oil producing and servicing firms such as: SHELL, SNEPCO, SEPLAT, NPDC, WARRI REF, Government and its agencies.


We have a team of seasoned Inspectors and drilling professionals who has vast experience working with Major Oil companies worldwide. Our Team has managing and operating Cargo Inspection and Testing projects Jack-up Rigs and DP Semi-Submersibles, Drillships, Barge Rigs in all water depths for the past 35 years.


Our team have managed Cargo Inspection and drilling operations almost on all continents of the globe! Our team can deliver the highest Safety and Operational standards in the industry! We will exceed the client’s expectations with our performance and HSE standards! Blackwell Petroleum Group is focused on ready high value offshore and onshore projects.


Blackwell Petroleum Group, with its strategic investors is able to make equity investments in both assets and mobilization in Testing equipment’s and drilling assets.


Blackwell Petroleum Ltd, We have strong foreign and local experience as we maintain a computerized Data Bank of well over 2000 oilfield personnel, who have been screened and short-listed, so that they can be available at short notice. This clearly gives us a competitive edge over the competition. This is achieved through having proper recruitment, remuneration, continual training where needed and performance appraisal systems in place.
